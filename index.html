Welcome to the Aliens and Spaceships Game!

You are a citizen of Earth. Recently, Earth has run out of rare gems and minerals. As an astronaut working for Secret Government Agency (SGA), you are sent to find valuable gems far away in the galaxy!

Throughout this game, each gem you find will earn you points. For this game, your mission director has allowed you 150 trips into space to collect as many valuable gems as possible.

At the end of this game, you will earn a monetary bonus based on how many points you collected during your mission. Good luck!

Press any key to continue.
